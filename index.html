Welcome To
Mount Zion Bible Baptist Church

We are Independent, Fundamental Bible Baptist Church that believe what the Bible says. By independent we mean we are Baptist by conviction and principle rather than by affiliation with a particular denomination. Because of this, we can support our own missionaries, select our own leaders, and support institutions that believe as we believe. In short, our church answers to the headship of Jesus Christ rather than a denominational body.

Pastor Adrian Codilla

MZBBC Church Pastor
